Our Legacy — Est. 2026

Roasting with Intention, Brewing with Precision

We started with a simple obsession: sourcing the finest micro-lot beans and roasting them to uncover their natural, explosive flavor profiles.

From origin to execution, our process is built on absolute transparency and technical dedication. We work directly with sustainable farms to secure unique coffee varietals, which are then custom-profiled in small batches at our workspace. Every cup we serve is an invitation to explore the true, unadulterated character of the coffee cherry.

The Craft Pillars

🌍

Ethical Sourcing

We build direct-trade partnerships with independent micro-lots. By bypassing traditional industrial supply lines, we guarantee stable premiums for growers and absolute clarity on crop seasons.

🔥

Batch Roasting

Instead of automated mass-browning, our roasters manipulate air velocity and thermodynamic development curves by hand, pinpointing the perfect highlight parameters for each bean matrix.

🧪

Scientific Extraction

Water mineral ratios, precise extraction windows, and total dissolved solids are checked daily. We treat bar curation as an exact laboratory discipline to protect structural flavor profiles.
